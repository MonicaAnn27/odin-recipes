Ramen

Ramen is a great dish anytime of year, it's both delicious and nutrious. This dish can be customized to whatever you're craving or what you might have in the fridge. It can be served as vegetarian or with your favorite protein. Follow your heart.

Ingredients

  • 1 thumb size bunch of ramen noodles
  • 2 cups broth (bone or veggie)
  • 1 baby bok choy
  • light soy sauce
  • hon tsyu
  • 1 shredded carrot
  • 1 soft boiled egg
  • pickled jalapenos
  • scallion
  • lime

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta and the egg
  2. As you warm up the broth add the soy sauce and hon tsyu to taste
  3. Cup the baby bok choy and put it in the bottom of a large soup bowl
  4. Once the noodles are cooked, strain and put them on top of the bok choy
  5. Next pour your hot brother over the top
  6. Pile on the ingredients! Enjoy the warmth that fills your belly and soul
