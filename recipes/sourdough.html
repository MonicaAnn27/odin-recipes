Sourdough

Fresh bread is one of the most miraculous things on the planet. This is the way.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams active sourdough starter
  • 350 grams room temperature water
  • 500 grams flour
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Add the active sourdough starter to your bowl
  2. Pour the room temperature water over your starter
  3. Mix the starter and water thoroughly
  4. Add in the salt and stir
  5. Add the flour
  6. Mix throughly until it forms a dough ball
  7. Cover and let it rise overnight or for 8 hours
  8. Dump the dough onto a floured surface and shape our dough ball
  9. Place it in the proofing basket and cover it for one hour
  10. Turn your oven on to 500 degrees
  11. Put the dough in your bread pan and place it in the oven. Cook it at 475 for 20 min
  12. Uncover your bread and cook for another 15 minutes
  13. Take it out of the oven and place it on the cooling rack and listen to the cool noises it makes
  14. Try not to devour the whole loaf, but it's ok if you do
